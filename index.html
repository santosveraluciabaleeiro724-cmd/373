About JQC-機構帳戶

Company Overview

Founded and registered in England in 2018, JQC-Institutional Account is a leading independent financial information consultancy firm dedicated exclusively to serving institutional investors, asset management firms, corporate finance divisions and cross-border investment organizations. Our headquarters is strategically located at 64 Pier St, Kingston upon Hull, UK — a hub for international financial services in Northern England — and we employ a multidisciplinary team of 28 senior financial analysts, former regulatory specialists, and global market researchers with average industry experience of 12+ years.

Unlike general financial advisory firms, we focus solely on institutional clients, allowing us to develop deep expertise in the unique challenges and opportunities faced by large-scale investors, fund managers, and corporate treasury departments operating in global markets. Our client roster includes pension funds, hedge funds, asset management companies, and multinational corporations across Europe, Asia, and North America.


Our Mission

To supply transparent, reliable and actionable financial intelligence to institutional clients, lowering information asymmetry and optimizing long-term investment portfolio performance under compliant global financial frameworks. We aim to be the trusted partner for institutional clients seeking to navigate complex regulatory environments, identify market inefficiencies, and build resilient investment strategies that align with their risk appetite and long-term objectives.


Our Development Journey

  • 2018 - Company established in Kingston upon Hull, focusing on UK/EU institutional account consulting
  • 2020 - Expanded service scope to include cross-border financial consulting for Asian institutional clients
  • 2022 - Launched proprietary market risk assessment model for institutional portfolios
  • 2024 - Opened strategic partnership with European financial data providers, enhancing real-time market intelligence capabilities
